# Lagdata

## Repository Context

This repository focuses on aggregating technical information about the Swedish legal information system (rättsinformationssystemet). The primary audience includes developers, data scientists, and civic tech enthusiasts who want to access, parse, and reuse Swedish legal data.

## Method

We use lagrummet.se as the frame for what is considered Swedish legal information. A Markdown copy of the website's content is available at [sources/lagrummet.se](./sources/lagrummet.se/). We look at the same type of documents they describe with the same categories (*förarbeten*, *lagar och förordningar*, *rättspraxis*, *myndigheters föreskrifter*). We keep a TO-DO in Swedish in the README file of what is described in this project.

## Guidelines for AI Agents

1. **Technical Focus:** Prioritize the technical aspects of the data (APIs, endpoints, data formats like XML/JSON, database schemas, and data extraction guidelines) over legal interpretation or legal advice.
2. **Accuracy with Terminology:** Maintain strict accuracy when using Swedish legal terminology (e.g., *Svensk författningssamling (SFS)*, *Förarbeten*, *Vägledande avgöranden*). When explaining concepts in English, provide the original Swedish term to maintain clarity.
3. **Objective Tone:** Keep a professional, objective, and neutral tone appropriate for a project dealing with official government and legal data.
4. **No Legal Advice:** Never generate content that could be construed as legal advice. The focus is strictly on data availability and engineering.
5. **Source Verification:** When researching or providing examples, prioritize and cite official Swedish government sources, such as `lagrummet.se`, `data.riksdagen.se`, or the official portals of specific authorities and courts.
